VIRGIN ISLANDS 
STATUTORY INSTRUMENT 2024 NO. 47 
ANTI-MONEY LAUNDERING (AMENDMENT) REGULATIONS, 2024 
(S.I. No. 43 of 2024) 
A NOTICE 
MADE BY THE MINISTER FOR FINANCIAL SERVICES, LABOUR AND TRADE 
UNDER REGULATION 1(2) OF THE ANTI-MONEY LAUNDERING (AMENDMENT) 
REGULATIONS, 2024 DECLARING THE DATE ON WHICH THE SAID 
REGULATIONS SHALL COME INTO FORCE 
 
 
[Gazetted 22nd October, 2024] 
 
 
WHEREAS it is provided by regulation 1(2) of the Anti-money Laundering (Amendment) 
Regulations, 2024 (S.I. No. 43 of 2024) that the said Regulations shall come into force on such 
date as the Minister may, by Notice published in the Gazette, appoint; 
 
NOW THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers vested in me, I hereby appoint the 15th 
day of October, 2024 to be the date on which the Anti-money Laundering (Amendment) 
Regulations, 2024 shall come into force. 
 
 
 
 
 
Made by the Minister this 15th day of October, 2024. 
 
 
 
 
(Sgd.) Lorna G. Smith,  
  Minister for Financial Services, Labour and Trade.

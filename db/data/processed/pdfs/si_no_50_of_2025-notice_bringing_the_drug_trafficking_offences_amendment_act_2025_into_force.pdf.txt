VIRGIN ISLANDS 
STATUTORY INSTRUMENT 2025 NO. 50 
 
DRUG TRAFFICKING OFFENCES (AMENDMENT) ACT, 2025  
(NO. 8 OF 2025) 
 
A NOTICE 
BY HIS EXCELLENCY THE GOVERNOR UNDER SECTION 1(2) OF THE DRUG 
TRAFFICKING OFFENCES (AMENDMENT) ACT, 2025 DECLARING THE DATE 
ON WHICH THE SAID ACT SHALL COME INTO FORCE 
 
 
 
(Sgd.) Daniel Pruce, 
Governor. 
 
[Gazetted 6th June, 2025] 
 
WHEREAS it is provided by section 1(2) of the Drug Trafficking Offences 
(Amendment) Act, 2025 (No. 8 of 2025) that the said Act shall come into force on such date 
as the Governor may, by Notice published in the Gazette, appoint;  
 
NOW THEREFORE, by the virtue and in exercise of the powers vested in me, I do 
hereby proclaim that the Drug Trafficking Offences (Amendment) Act, 2025 shall come into 
force on the 6th day of June, 2025.  
 
AND all His Majesty’s loving subjects are hereby required to take note hereof and 
govern themselves accordingly.  
 
 
GIVEN under my hand 
and the public seal of the Virgin 
Islands at the Governor’s Office in 
Tortola in the Virgin Islands this 
6th day of June, 2025 and in the 
third year of His Majesty’s reign.  
 
 
GOD SAVE THE KING!

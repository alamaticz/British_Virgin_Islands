VIRGIN ISLANDS 
STATUTORY INSTRUMENT 2025 NO. 58 
 
PROCEEDS OF CRIMINAL CONDUCT (AMENDMENT) ACT, 2025  
(No. 7 of 2025) 
 
A NOTICE 
MADE BY THE MINISTER OF FINANCE UNDER SECTION 1(2) OF THE PROCEEDS 
OF CRIMINAL CONDUCT (AMENDMENT) ACT, 2025 DECLARING THE DATE ON 
WHICH THE SAID ACT SHALL COME INTO FORCE 
 
 
[Gazetted 20th June, 2025] 
 
 
WHEREAS it is provided by section 1(2) of the Proceeds of Criminal Conduct 
(Amendment) Act, 2025 (No. 7 of 2025) that the said Act shall come into force on such date as 
the Minister may, by Notice published in the Gazette, appoint; 
 
NOW THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers vested in me, I hereby appoint the 6th day 
of June, 2025 to be the date on which the Proceeds of Criminal Conduct (Amendment) Act, 2025 
shall come into force. 
 
 
 
 
 
Made by the Minister this 6th day of June, 2025. 
 
 
 
 
(Sgd.) Natalio D. Wheatley, 
Minister of Finance.

 
VIRGIN ISLANDS 
 
STATUTORY INSTRUMENT 2024 NO. 73  
 
PROLIFERATION FINANCING (PROHIBITION) (AMENDMENT) ACT, 2024 
(No. 20 of 2024) 
 
A NOTICE 
 
MADE BY THE MINISTER OF FINANCIAL SERVICES, ECONOMIC 
DEVELOPMENT AND DIGITAL TRANSFORMATION 
UNDER SECTION 1(2) OF THE PROLIFERATION FINANCING (PROHIBITION) 
(AMENDMENT) ACT, 2024 DECLARING THE DATE ON WHICH THE SAID ACT 
SHALL COME INTO FORCE 
 
[Gazetted 27th December, 2024] 
 
 
WHEREAS it is provided by section 1(2) of the Proliferation Financing (Prohibition) 
(Amendment) Act, 2024 (No. 20 of 2024) that the said Act shall come into force on such date as 
the Minister may, by Notice published in the Gazette, appoint; 
 
 
NOW THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers vested in me, I hereby appoint the 2nd day 
of January, 2025 to be the date on which the Proliferation Financing (Prohibition) (Amendment) 
Act, 2024 shall come into force. 
 
 
Made by the Minister this 19th day of December, 2024. 
 
 
 
 
(Sgd.) Natalio D. Wheatley, 
Minister of Financial Services, Economic  
Development and Digital Transformation.

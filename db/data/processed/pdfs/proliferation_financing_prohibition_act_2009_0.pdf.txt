An Act to confer powers on the Financial Investigation Agency to take action against 
persons and activities that may be related to terrorist financing, money laundering or the 
development of weapons of mass destruction and for other connected matters. 
 
 
[Gazetted 19th November, 2009] 
 
 
ENACTED by the Legislature of the Virgin Islands as follows:

financial sector; and  
 
(g)
